Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how FRCOphtn Pro collects, stores, and protects any information you provide while using our website. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal information remains secure at all times.

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us — we will never sell, trade, or distribute your email address or any other personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Information We Collect

When you create an account or make a purchase on FRCOphtn Pro, we collect and securely store the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address

During your use of the website, we also record the following data to personalise your learning experience and maintain platform integrity:

  • Your exam history and performance statistics
  • Comments and notes you have made on questions
  • Messages or feedback you have submitted to the admin team
  • Login details including date, time, and IP address

All user data is stored securely on protected servers. We do not collect, process, or store any payment card information. All transactions are handled separately through trusted third-party payment providers such as Stripe and PayPal.

User Interaction and Displayed Information

Users have the option to participate in discussion sections or comment threads within the website. Your public screen name defaults to the first part of your email address, but you can change it at any time. Updating your screen name will automatically update all past and future comments linked to your account.

Email Communication

We aim to keep email communication to a minimum. However, you may occasionally receive messages from us for the following purposes:

  • Welcoming new subscribers or confirming registration
  • Assisting with login, password reset, or account-related queries
  • Responding to user feedback or technical issues
  • Sharing information about site updates or improvements relevant to your studies

Disclosure of Information

If FRCOphtn Pro believes that your use of the site is unlawful, abusive, or potentially harmful to other users, we reserve the right to disclose your information when necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding such activity.

Your IP address may be used to identify you, gather general demographic statistics, and assist in diagnosing server or security issues. In cases of suspected fraud or misuse, we may provide relevant information to appropriate authorities.

Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of any personal information that we hold about you. To do so, please contact us at contact@frcophthpro.com.

Cookies and Analytics

FRCOphtn Pro uses analytics tools such as Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors use our site and to improve user experience. These tools may place cookies on your device to collect anonymous browsing data. You can disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

Security of Your Information

We employ modern security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of online transmission or electronic storage is entirely secure, and therefore we cannot guarantee absolute security.
