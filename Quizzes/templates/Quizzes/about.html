About Us

FRCOphth Pro is a modern, high-quality revision platform built specifically for the FRCOphth Part 1 examination. Our mission is to make ophthalmology exam preparation clear, structured, and affordable for every trainee.

At the core of FRCOphth Pro is a curated set of high-yield, syllabus-mapped questions designed to mirror the real exam format. Each question is paired with concise yet detailed explanations, allowing you to master both key concepts and finer details with efficiency.

All questions are written and reviewed by practising ophthalmologists and clinicians with real exam experience. We maintain a strict policy of quality over quantity — every question is relevant, up-to-date, and aligned with current FRCOphth standards. We never pad our database with filler content.

Performance data and user feedback are continuously analysed, ensuring that underperforming or outdated content is refined or removed. Our goal is to provide the most accurate, evidence-based revision experience available online.

We understand how expensive postgraduate exams can be. That’s why FRCOphth Pro is committed to offering premium-quality resources at an affordable cost. We rely on organic growth and recommendations from satisfied users rather than costly advertising campaigns.

FRCOphth Pro isn’t a “one-stop” shortcut to success — but used wisely, it can dramatically increase your chances of passing. Combine it with good study habits, clinical exposure, and advice from successful candidates for the best results.

If you’d like to get in touch, share feedback, or suggest new features, feel free to contact us.

Dr Abdel Mageed
Founder & Managing Director
